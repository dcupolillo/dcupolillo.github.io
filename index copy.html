About Me

Hello, I'm Dario!

Postdoc in neuroscience @ IIT

Based in Genoa . With a background in biotechnology, I pursued a Ph.D. in neuroscience to study how neuron physiology impacts memory (University of Bordeaux ). Now, in my postdoc, I use calcium imaging to study dendritic spines while expanding my skills in data and image analysis, programming, and hardware development. Currently, I am exploring machine learning approaches. I am excited to find out where this journey will lead.
